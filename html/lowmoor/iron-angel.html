The Iron Angel

In Lowmoor settlement Jewel and Thorn discover a conspiracy which threatens everything they know, and which they alone can thwart. In order to enhance already remarkable powers, Querne Rasp seeks to complete her own collection of crystals and wield the five-stone crystal star, with its dark vortex. Meanwhile, the enigmatic Racky Jagger approaches a crisis of his own…

Jewel and Thorn's most dangerous quest yet takes them into a ruined giant ironworks where a fanatical tribe of humans wages unending war on rats. Then on to a Dark Time church inhabited by a sinister cult. And finally into the bowels of the church, where their courage and ingenuity will be tested to the limit.
