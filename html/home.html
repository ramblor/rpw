Welcome to my website!

My big work is the Book of Lowmoor, an epic fantasy in three parts. Volume 1, Jewel and Thorn, is now out from Simon & Schuster, and is available from bookshops and on the web. For information on the trilogy, including background and early reviews, click on the Book of Lowmoor link.

For press and online reactions to Jewel and Thorn, click on Stop Press - Latest News.

Younger readers - including watchers of the Nick Jr TV channel - should click on their own link. This leads to Teeny Tiny Creatures, animations of short, sharp poems.

My five books of poems are listed under the Poetry section. Each collection is represented by one poem and extracts from contemporary reviews.

The Criticism section will in the future reproduce some of the literary essays that I've written over the years. There will be a large section on Richard Hughes, and individual essays on Philip Pullman, Thomas Harris, Vladimir Nabokov and many poets including Shakespeare, Dylan Thomas and R. S. Thomas.

Enjoy!
