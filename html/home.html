THE BOOK OF LOWMOOR

In a world shaped by the scattered remains of a giant civilisation and dangerous blank areas where nothing exists live communities of tiny humans. Small in stature but big in dreams, Jewel and Thorn separately embark on a series of thrilling adventures…

The Book of Lowmoor is an epic dark fantasy novel in three parts. Designed to be enjoyed by readers from 10 to 110, it was published between 2005 and 2008 in hardback and paperback by Simon & Schuster. Book 1 is called Jewel and Thorn, Book 2 The Brass Key, and Book 3 The Iron Angel. Click on the links for descriptions of the books, extracts from reviews and links to bookselling websites.

POETRY

My poetry has appeared in magazines and anthologies in Wales, England, Ireland and the U.S.A. I have published five collections of poetry: Goings and other poems, Words before Midnight, Natural Histories, Autobiographies and Explorations and, most recently, Camelion (2012). Also a chapbook of translations from the twentieth century Welsh poetry T. H. Parry-Williams entitled That Fool July.
