Fiction as Truth: Selected Literary Writings by Richard Hughes
Edited by Richard Poole (Poetry Wales Press 1983)


Fiction as Truth is a selection of essays, forewords, and book reviews by Richard Hughes. In most cases they come from a variety of publications, many defunct, but a few are previously unpublished. The selection spans fifty years of the novelist's writing life. Its contents are divided into four sections bracketed by an Introduction by the editor and a Bibliography.
